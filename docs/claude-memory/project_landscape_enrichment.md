# Landscape Enrichment Plan

## The Principle
Intelligence entries and landscape profiles are the SAME ecosystem. Intelligence entries are evidence. Landscape profiles synthesize that evidence into a coherent AI strategy narrative per company.

## What Each Company Profile Needs
For each of the 37 companies in data/competitors/:

1. **AI Strategy Summary** — rewrite in consulting voice using ALL our intelligence entries + fresh research. Not a feature list. A strategic assessment of where this firm stands, what they're building, and why it matters.

2. **Capability Headlines** — each capability (advisor_productivity, client_personalization, etc.) needs a specific, evidence-backed headline. Not "deploying AI" but "98% advisor adoption, 30 min saved per meeting via Debrief (Morgan Stanley press release, June 2024)."

3. **Evidence Arrays** — every evidence line must link to a source. Use our verified intelligence entries as primary evidence. Add fresh sources from web research.

4. **Maturity Assessments** — verify each maturity level (scaled/deployed/piloting/announced) against current evidence. Some may have advanced since last assessment.

5. **Cross-Reference with Intelligence Feed** — for each company, pull all our published intelligence entries and ensure the landscape profile reflects the latest verified developments.

## Sources Available
- Our own intelligence entries (43 entries, multi-source verified)
- Our own thought leadership entries (8 entries)
- Fresh web research (WebSearch for latest developments)
- Company newsrooms and press releases
- Trade press (ThinkAdvisor, InvestmentNews, WealthManagement.com, etc.)
