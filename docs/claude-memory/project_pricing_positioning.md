# Pricing & Positioning — Thinking (2026-04-01)

## Key Insight: This is a firm spend, not a personal subscription

A CEO won't put $500/month on their card for an intelligence platform. But a firm will approve $10,000/year if the strategy head or head of digital expenses it as a competitive intelligence tool for the leadership team.

**The shift:** B2C individual subscription → B2B team/firm license

## Pricing Direction

- **Single annual fee** — no monthly option at this tier
- **~$10,000/year for 1 seat** (individual access)
- **~$15,000/year for 3 seats** (team — e.g., CEO + Head of Digital + Head of Strategy)
- Possibly higher tiers for larger teams (5-10 seats)
- Annual billing only — this is an enterprise expense, not a consumer subscription

## Sales Flow (Haresh's thinking)

1. **Trial access** — let them see the platform, fall in love with it
2. **Justification email** — automated or templated email that explains why this platform is worth $10K and helps the buyer get CFO approval
   - What the platform does (in simple terms)
   - Why this intelligence can't be assembled manually
   - The AI stack behind it (multi-source, fabrication-checked, scored)
   - ROI framing: "one missed competitive signal costs more than the annual fee"
3. **Simple purchase** — see it, love it, buy it, payment done. No sales calls.

## Why This Works

- CXOs don't buy $500/month tools — that's a personal expense category
- $10K/year is a departmental budget line item — approved by a team lead or VP
- Team access (3-5 seats) makes the firm invested, not just one person
- Annual commitment = predictable revenue, lower churn
- The "expense it" framing removes the personal spending friction

## What Needs to Change

- **Landing page (livingintel.ai):** Reposition from $450-500/month individual → $10K+ annual firm license
- **Pricing section:** Single clean tier (or 2-3 tiers based on seats), annual pricing, "Request Trial" CTA
- **CFO justification email/page:** Template or downloadable one-pager
- **Auth/access:** Support multiple users per account (team seats)
- **Stripe:** Annual billing, possibly invoicing for enterprise

## Previous Pricing (being replaced)

- $500/month standard
- These were B2C individual subscription prices — no longer the direction

## Research Completed (2026-04-01)

Full report at `living-intelligence/pricing-research.md` — 12 platforms researched with real pricing data.

### Key Findings
- Every comparable platform hides pricing behind "Contact Sales"
- The $20K-$50K/yr range is where most single-team CI purchases land
- AlphaSense: $10K-$20K/seat/yr, enterprise avg $125K. CB Insights: $50K-$265K+. Gartner: $25K-$500K+.
- At $10K-$25K price point, often a discretionary purchase (2-6 week cycle, no RFP needed)
- Firm-based licensing (not per-seat) recommended — eliminates adoption friction
- "Founding Member" program is the proven playbook for new B2B intelligence platforms
- Gated sample intelligence (not free trial) is the right conversion model

### DECIDED PRICING (2026-04-01, final)
- **Standard:** $5,000/year — firm license, 5 users
- **Founding Member:** $4,500/year — first 50 firms, rate locked
- **No monthly option.** Annual contracts only.
- B2B corporate purchase. 5 users per license (LinkedIn IDs).
- Sales routed through Singapore company (already set up).

## Distribution Research (2026-04-01)

Full report at `research_distribution.md`

### The $5K Threshold — Critical Finding
- Under $5K at most banks = credit card / discretionary spend. No procurement. 1-3 week cycle.
- $5K-$10K = grey zone. Large banks OK, mid-tier may trigger PO process.
- $10K+ = almost always needs purchase order, budget approval, vendor onboarding. 2-4 weeks added.
- Sales cycle: $5K ACV = 14-30 days. $10K ACV = 30-60 days. **2-3x faster at $5K.**

### Solo Founder Sales Stack (~$210/mo)
- Apollo.io ($99/mo) — contact database + email sequencing
- Instantly.ai ($30/mo) — email warmup + sending
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator ($80-100/mo) — prospect finding
- Calendly (free) — scheduling

### Realistic Numbers (solo founder, 2hrs/day selling)
- 200 cold outreaches/month → 1-3 closes/month
- At $5K: $60K-$180K ARR year one
- Hire first salesperson at $300K-$500K ARR (60-100 customers)

### LinkedIn
- $1,000-$1,500/mo budget for paid (Lead Gen Forms + Sponsored Content)
- Target: Head of Digital/AI/Strategy at 1000+ employee financial services firms
- Organic posting (free) is highest ROI: 3-5x/week, intelligence insights = free product demo

### Distribution Strategy (stealth mode)
- No personal LinkedIn. Brand "Living Intelligence" is the face.
- Referral partners with 20% commission — find well-connected people in WM space
- Warm introductions model: "Can you connect me to X?"
- Cold email from company identity (hello@livingintel.ai), not personal
- LinkedIn Company Page can post (no personal profile needed)
- AI-assisted outreach via Apollo/Instantly from company email
- Once at 10-20 customers, reassess visibility constraints
