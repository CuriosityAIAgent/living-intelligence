## 1. LinkedIn as Primary Channel

### LinkedIn Paid Advertising Costs (Financial Services Executives)

**CPM and CPC benchmarks for financial services targeting:**
- LinkedIn average CPC across all industries: $5-$7. For financial services executive targeting, expect **$8-$15 CPC** due to the narrow, high-value audience.
- CPM (cost per 1,000 impressions): LinkedIn average is $30-$35. For financial services C-suite/VP+ targeting, expect **$45-$80 CPM**.
- These are significantly higher than other platforms (Facebook CPC ~$1-2, Google Display ~$2-4) but the audience quality is incomparably better for this use case.

**LinkedIn Ad Formats — What Works at $5K-$10K ACV:**

| Format | Cost | Best For | Recommendation |
|--------|------|----------|----------------|
| Sponsored Content (feed posts) | $8-15 CPC | Awareness, thought leadership amplification | **Primary format** — most natural, least intrusive |
| Sponsored InMail (Message Ads) | $0.30-$1.00 per send | Direct offers, event invitations | **Good for demos** — 20-30% open rate typical, 2-5% CTR |
| Lead Gen Forms | $15-$40 per lead | Gated content (reports, benchmarks) | **Best for capture** — pre-filled forms reduce friction dramatically |
| Conversation Ads | $0.30-$0.80 per send | Multi-step engagement | Decent but feels automated; skip as a solo founder |
| Text Ads | $3-$6 CPC | Cheap awareness | Low impact, low CTR (0.02%), skip |

**Budget Tiers and What They Buy:**

- **$500/month:** ~50-60 clicks. Enough to test messaging and audience segments. Not enough for consistent pipeline. This is a learning budget, not a growth budget.
- **$1,000-$1,500/month:** ~100-150 clicks. Can run 2-3 campaigns, A/B test creative. Enough to generate 5-10 leads/month with strong content. This is the **minimum viable LinkedIn budget** for this use case.
- **$2,000-$3,000/month:** ~200-350 clicks. Meaningful pipeline. Can combine Sponsored Content (awareness) + Lead Gen Forms (capture) + retargeting. Expected: 15-30 leads/month.
- **$5,000/month:** Overkill for a solo founder who can only handle so many conversations. Save this for after product-market fit is confirmed.

**Practical recommendation:** Start at $1,000/month. Use Sponsored Content to amplify your best organic posts (the ones that already get engagement). Run Lead Gen Forms offering a free sample intelligence briefing. Target: "Head of Digital" OR "Head of AI" OR "Chief Strategy Officer" OR "Head of Innovation" at companies with 1,000+ employees in Financial Services. That audience on LinkedIn is probably 15,000-40,000 people globally.

**Case studies / patterns:**
- CB Insights (competitive intelligence, $50K+ ACV) built its brand almost entirely on LinkedIn organic + newsletter before scaling paid. Their model: publish contrarian research, build audience, convert subscribers to demos.
- AlphaSense, Visible Alpha, and Tegus all use LinkedIn Sponsored Content heavily to push "benchmark report" style gated content, then nurture via email.
- The pattern that works for B2B intelligence: **give away the insight, sell the access.** Post the finding on LinkedIn, gate the full report, nurture the download into a demo.

## 2. Other Distribution Channels for Solo Founders

### Cold Email

**Tools and realistic expectations:**

| Tool | Cost | Best For |
|------|------|----------|
| **Apollo.io** | $49-$99/mo | All-in-one: database + sequencing + enrichment. Best starting point. 200M+ contacts. |
| **Instantly.ai** | $30-$97/mo | High-volume sending with email warmup. Pair with Apollo for data. |
| **Lemlist** | $59-$99/mo | Personalization at scale (image/video personalization). |
| **Clay** | $149-$349/mo | Enrichment + AI personalization. Powerful but expensive for early stage. |
| **Smartlead** | $39-$94/mo | Alternative to Instantly, good warmup and rotation. |

**Response rates for financial services cold email:**
- Average cold email reply rate across B2B: 3-5%
- Financial services executives: **1-3% reply rate**
- With strong personalization (reference their firm's specific AI initiative): **3-7% reply rate**
- With a warm trigger (they just posted about AI on LinkedIn, firm announced AI initiative): **5-10% reply rate**

**Realistic cold email math for solo founder:**
- Send 200 targeted emails/week (40/day, highly personalized)
- 2-3% reply rate = 4-6 replies/week
- 50% positive = 2-3 conversations/week
- 30% of conversations to demo = ~1 demo/week = ~4 demos/month
- At 25% close rate = **1 deal/month from cold email alone**

### Organic LinkedIn / Content Marketing

**Highest-ROI channel** as a solo founder. Zero cost, compounds over time.

- Post 3-5x per week. Mix of: original analysis, contrarian takes on wealth management technology, "behind the scenes" of building the product.
- The content IS the product demo. Every post should make executives think "I need this analysis regularly."
- Format: short text posts outperform articles. 150-300 word posts with a hook, insight, and question.
- **Timeline to meaningful traction:** 3-6 months of consistent posting to build 2,000-5,000 relevant followers.

### Partnerships

- **Consultancies:** Find individual consultants covering wealth management, offer free access for referrals. One well-connected consultant = 3-5 intros.
- **Analyst firms (Celent, Aite-Novarica):** Being cited in a Celent report is worth more than $10K in advertising.
- **Technology vendors:** Co-marketing with wealth management platform vendors. Joint webinars.

### Product Hunt / Directories

**Skip.** Your buyers are not on Product Hunt or G2. Exception: **Chartis Research**, **Celent**, or **Bobsguide** (financial services directories).

## 3. The Price Sensitivity Question

### Procurement Thresholds at Banks

| Threshold | What Happens |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Under $5,000** | Credit card / discretionary. No procurement. No vendor onboarding. **The magic number.** |
| **$5,000-$10,000** | Grey zone. Large banks OK, mid-tier may trigger PO process. |
| **$10,000-$25,000** | Almost always needs PO, budget approval, possibly vendor onboarding. Adds 2-4 weeks. |
| **$25,000-$50,000** | Formal procurement. Security questionnaire. Legal review. 1-3 months added. |
| **$50,000+** | Full procurement cycle. RFP possible. 3-6+ months. |

**Sales cycle by price:**
- $1K-$5K ACV: average 14-30 days
- $5K-$15K ACV: average 30-60 days
- $15K-$50K ACV: average 60-120 days

At $5K, close in 2-3 conversations. At $10K, expect 4-6 conversations and "let me check with my manager."

## 4. AI-Powered Sales Automation

### Solo Founder Sales Stack (~$210/month)

| Tool | Cost | Purpose |
|------|------|---------|
| **Apollo.io** (Professional) | $99/mo | Contact database + email sequencing + CRM |
| **Instantly.ai** (Growth) | $30/mo | Email warmup + high-volume sending |
| **Calendly** (free) | $0 | Meeting scheduling |
| **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** | $80-$100/mo | Advanced search + InMail + lead tracking |

### AI Agents for Follow-Up

- **Clay + Claude API:** Research prospect, draft personalized email, queue for review.
- **n8n + Claude API:** Trigger on new LinkedIn connection, enrich, draft follow-up, queue. Cost: $20-$50/month.
- **Key rule:** Automate research and drafting. Do NOT automate sending to bank executives without review.

### Minimum Viable Sales Process

1. **Identify** (30 min/day): Find 5-10 prospects via Apollo/Sales Navigator
2. **Reach out** (30 min/day): 5-10 personalized emails + 3-5 LinkedIn requests
3. **Engage** (as needed): Reply within 4 hours. Offer sample briefing, not demo.
4. **Demo** (30 min): Show their firm's landscape. No slides, just the product.
5. **Close** (1 email): Annual subscription, pay by invoice or card. 30-day satisfaction guarantee.
6. **Onboard** (15 min): Quick call. Ask for one referral.

## 5. Founder-Led Sales Reality

### Conversion Funnel Benchmarks ($5K-$10K ACV)

| Stage | Conversion Rate |
|-------|----------------|
| Cold outreach to Reply | 2-5% |
| Reply to Qualified conversation | 40-60% |
| Conversation to Demo | 50-70% |
| Demo to Proposal | 60-80% |
| Proposal to Close | 25-40% |
| **End-to-end: Cold to Close** | **0.5-1.5%** |
| **Warm lead to Close** | **10-25%** |

### Deals Per Month (Solo Founder, 2hrs/day)

- **At $5K ACV:** 1-3 closes/month = $60K-$180K ARR year one
- **At $10K ACV:** 1-2 closes/month = $120K-$240K ARR potential but slower ramp
- **Realistic year one target:** 15-25 customers, $75K-$150K ARR

### When to Hire First Salesperson

- At **$300K-$500K ARR** (60-100 customers at $5K)
- You need a repeatable playbook first
- **Alternative:** Fractional SDR ($2K-$4K/month) or commission-only referral partners (15-20% of first year)

## Summary: The Solo Founder Playbook

**Highest ROI actions in order:**

1. **Organic LinkedIn** (free, 30 min/day): Post intelligence insights 3-5x/week
2. **Cold email with Apollo + Instantly** (~$130/month): 200 emails/month, 1-2 deals/month
3. **LinkedIn paid ads** ($1,000-$1,500/month): Amplify posts + Lead Gen Forms, 10-20 leads/month
4. **2-3 industry events/year** ($3K-$6K total): 1-2 deals per event
5. **Referral program** (free): 15-20% first-year commission

**Year one budget:** $15K-$25K total. Target: $75K-$150K ARR.
